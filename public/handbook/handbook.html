üìã Rules & Guidelines

Team Size

Teams must consist of 3-5 members. Solo participation is not allowed.

All code must be written during the hackathon. Pre-built solutions are not permitted.

Any programming language or framework is allowed. Use what you're comfortable with.

Submissions

All projects must be submitted by 10 AM on Day 3 via the submission portal.
